JPCS Membership Terms & Conditions

By joining the Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS), you agree to:

  • Abide by the rules and regulations of JPCS
  • Participate actively in organizational activities
  • Maintain good academic standing
  • Uphold the values and principles of JPCS
  • Pay membership dues as required
  • Contribute to the growth and development of the organization
  • Respect fellow members and officers
  • Attend required meetings and events

Membership may be revoked for violation of these terms.
