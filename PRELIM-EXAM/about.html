About JPCS

Shaping the future of ICT education and professional development in the Philippines.

Who We Are

The Junior Philippine Computer Society (JPCS) is the premier student organization dedicated to Information and Communications Technology (ICT) education in the Philippines. We serve as the youth arm of the Philippine Computer Society (PCS), connecting students with industry professionals and academic excellence.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world ICT applications, preparing students to become globally competitive professionals in the digital age.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To enhance ICT education by providing students with practical experiences that complement academic learning, fostering innovation, collaboration, and professional development that prepares them for successful careers in technology.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that shapes the future of ICT professionals in the Philippines, creating a vibrant community where students, academia, and industry collaborate to drive technological advancement and digital transformation.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative thinking and technological advancement in ICT solutions.

Collaboration

Building strong partnerships between students, schools, and industry leaders.

Excellence

Pursuing the highest standards in ICT education and professional development.

Community

Creating a supportive network that nurtures growth and lifelong connections.

Why JPCS Matters

For Students

JPCS transforms classroom knowledge into practical skills through:

  • Hands-on workshops and training sessions
  • National competitions and hackathons
  • Industry networking opportunities
  • Professional development programs
  • Access to the PCS professional network
  • Career guidance and mentorship

For Schools

JPCS enhances institutional reputation and educational quality by:

  • Showcasing student achievements nationally
  • Providing direct industry-academia linkages
  • Keeping curriculum relevant to industry needs
  • Creating opportunities for faculty development
  • Enhancing school visibility through competitions
  • Building stronger alumni networks
